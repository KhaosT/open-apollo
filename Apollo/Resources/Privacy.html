Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted by Apollo app on iOS and watchOS.


Information we collect

Apollo does no collect any information that identifies individual user. When interacting with backend services, we may collect information like IP address and request URL to help understand the app usage. Playback history are not collected in any form by Apollo.


Information Usage

We use the information we collect to operate and improve our website, and apps.


We do not share your personal information with outside party. We may share anonymous, aggregate statistics with outside parties, such as how many people listened music using Apollo.


We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property.


In the future, we may sell to, buy, merge with, or partner with other businesses. In such transactions, any information we have collected may be among the transferred assets.


Security

We implement a variety of security measures to help keep your information secure. The initially authorization between Spotify and Apollo is done over OAuth 2.0, and during process your credential is never exposed to Apollo. Once the authorization is granted, Apollo stores an encrypted refresh token in device keychain so it can request access token in the future.


California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.


Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.
